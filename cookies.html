Cookie Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026 · Applies to gymostool.xyz

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your preferences and understand how you use them.

What we use

GymOS uses a minimal set of cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to ad networks.

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
__stripe_mid Stripe Fraud prevention and payment security 1 year
__stripe_sid Stripe Payment session management Session
gymos_cookie_ok GymOS Remembers that you've accepted this notice 1 year

Strictly necessary cookies

The Stripe cookies above are strictly necessary for the payment checkout to work. They cannot be disabled without breaking the checkout flow. We do not use them for tracking or profiling.

Analytics

We currently do not run any analytics cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and ask for your consent.

How to manage cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling all cookies may prevent the Stripe checkout from functioning correctly.
