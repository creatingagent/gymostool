How it works

Simple. Automatic. Done.

Five steps, none of which require you to do anything.

1
Card declined
Stripe detects the failed payment the moment it happens and fires a webhook. GymOS picks it up instantly — no manual checking, no delays.
2
We wait 60 minutes
Stripe has its own automatic retry logic. We respect that. GymOS sits quietly for an hour. If Stripe recovers the payment on its own, we do nothing at all. We only step in when it's clear the retry didn't work.
3
WhatsApp sent
A polite, personal message goes to your member. It includes a direct link to Stripe's secure card-update page. No login required, no friction — just a 30-second fix from their phone.
4
Member updates their card
They tap the link, enter new card details on a Stripe-hosted page, and they're done. PCI compliant, encrypted, and fast. Most members do this within minutes of receiving the message.
5
Money recovered
Stripe retries the payment automatically. The charge goes through, the membership stays active, and you see the money land in your Stripe dashboard. You didn't need to do a thing.

Why it works

Five honest reasons.

Works with Stripe

If your gym software takes card payments, it almost certainly runs on Stripe underneath. GymOS plugs straight in — no complex integrations, no new payment provider to sign up for.

Uses WhatsApp

Open rates for WhatsApp messages are around 5× higher than email. Your members already have it open. That's just where people actually read things in 2026.

Waits before messaging

Stripe retries automatically. We only message if that retry fails. So your members never get a panicked message about a payment that was going to go through anyway.

10-minute setup

You share your Stripe ID. We handle everything else. No developer needed, no new software to learn. Most gyms are live the same day they sign up.

Already EU compliant

Every message includes the required disclosure under the EU AI Act, which takes full effect in August 2026. You're covered before most gyms even know it applies to them.
