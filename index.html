Why it works

Five honest reasons.

Works with Stripe
If your gym software takes card payments, it almost certainly runs on Stripe underneath. GymOS plugs straight in.
Uses WhatsApp
WhatsApp open rates are 5× higher than email. Your members already have it open. That's just where people read things in 2026.
Waits before messaging
Stripe retries automatically. We only message if that retry fails. No panicked messages about payments that were already going to clear.
10-minute setup
You share your Stripe ID. We handle everything else. No developer needed, no new software to learn. Most gyms are live the same day.
Already EU compliant
Every message includes the required AI disclosure under the EU AI Act (August 2026). You're covered before most gyms even know it applies.
